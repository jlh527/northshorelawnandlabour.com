About Us

North Shore Lawn & Labour is a locally owned, customer-first business founded and operated by Laine Hargarten and Zoe Pulfrey. We combine technical precision, hands-on landscaping experience, and strong organizational skills to deliver dependable, high-quality work for both residential and small commercial clients.

We take pride in clear communication, reliable scheduling, and respectful service. Whether it’s seasonal yard maintenance, clearing and organizing a garage or basement, or providing general labour for a one-off project, we handle the heavy lifting so you don’t have to.
