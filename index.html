About Us

North Shore Yard and Labour is a locally-owned business specializing in comprehensive yard maintenance, landscaping, home organizing, and labour services. Founded by cousins Laine and Zoe, we provide reliable, professional solutions for residential properties with a focus on quality work and customer satisfaction.

Whether you need help with seasonal yard work, organizing your garage or basement, or general handyman services, we're here to handle the work so you can enjoy your space.

🌿 Yard Maintenance

Regular upkeep, mowing, trimming, weeding, and seasonal care to keep your outdoor space looking its best.

🌳 Landscaping

🧹 Seasonal Services

Spring cleanups, fall leaf removal, snow management, and holiday preparation.

🏠 Garage Organizing
