About Us

North Shore Yard and Labour is a locally-owned business specializing in comprehensive yard maintenance, landscaping, home organizing, and labour services. Founded by cousins Laine and Zoe, we bring professionalism, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence to every project.

Whether you need help with seasonal yard work, organizing your garage or basement, or general handyman services, we're here to handle the work so you can enjoy your space.
